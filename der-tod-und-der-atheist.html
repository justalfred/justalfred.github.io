Der Tod und Der Atheist

Date (Sat) 2016-01-30 Tags humans

Inspired by Schubert’s lied.

The Atheist (me, hopefully not any time soon):

Not just yet! Oh, not just yet!
There is so much left to learn!
I have obligations to fulfill!
Let me do more good.
Let me do more good.

Death (also me):

Give me your hand, you fragile, defeatist soul!
I am a friend, and come not to judge.
Your debts are paid! You’ve earned friends’ love,
Surrender your sensations to serenity!
