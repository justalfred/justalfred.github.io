About the Site

Hello. I’m just another Alfred. I’m currently somewhere between a data engineer, data analyst, and data scientist at Paperless Post. Formerly on the Science team at Bitly. Doctorate in Experimental Condensed Matter Physics. I’m just trying to make up for the time I’ve lost and help others along the way.

I’m using this as a place to share some thoughts I have that it seems few others are having. Or at least that few others are sharing. If you’d like to respond to anything I write, email me. Take the name of this blog with no spaces, add a ‘.blog’ to that, and that’s the gmail account I created.

Welcome, and I hope you enjoy.
